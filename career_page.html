Pioneer in Healthcare, Research & Teaching

San jose st. bonaventure Hospitals delivers services to many residents living not just in Gujrat or India but all across the world through its 6 state of the art facilities at Ahmedabad, Vadodara, Rajkot and Gandhidham. The remarkable range of specialties places us at the forefront of career destinations for Doctors, Nurses, Paramedics, Support function professionals and allied professionals from across the country. Our amazing workforce and remarkable organizational culture ensures that San jose st. bonaventure is always promoting better health for our communities through innovation and exceptional care. We strongly believe in Skill Enhancement & Development of the employees from across the hospital. We heavily focus on training our employees, rewarding and retaining them in our hospital. With the availability of all the possible logistical amenities required for training the medical as well as non-medical staff, we continuously strive on moving our employees towards personal growth and perfection.

How We Care For Our Employees

In adherence to our culture & values, we ensure that during the various life cycle stages of our employees in the hospital, they experience comfort, safety and a pleasant work environment. As our vision depicts a culture of care & compassion, we provide an easy work environment, inducting our new hires about the hospital and supporting them throughout their journey with us. Our culture sets us apart by focusing on being a friendly employer towards our workforce and extending an easy access to all levels of management. Safety of our employees is of prime importance towards which we extend our health benefits by offering them Vaccinations, Consultation and Annual Health checkups for free. Our well defined employee and contractual staff discount policy for hospitalization extends benefits to their families even.

    • Culture & Diversity

    Diversity is an integral part of who we are, how we operate and how we see the future. Our team comes together to celebrate diverse festivals, which creates a platform for our employees to come together and show their talent through different competitions and activities arranged during these festivals.

    Workplace Safety For Women

    As our hospitals functions round the clock, we always ensure that our women employees are working in a secured environment. Our surveillance team is on their guard 24/7 safeguarding our patients and employees across the hospitals.

    Easy Interaction

    Our open door policy across the functions and levels allows our employees to express their opinions and interact with each other on a regular basis. We also have departmental meetings on regular basis to discuss on the areas of functional improvement. Our doctors have a knowledge sharing forum – Thursday Clinical Meet – on a weekly basis, where they meet, discuss and brainstorm on the enhancements in the medical services.

    Forum for Medical Professionals:

    At San jose st. bonaventure Hospitals, we regularly conduct seminars, training programs and various meets which assembles Medical Professionals & Specialists from various fields and corners of the world.
